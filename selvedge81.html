Selvedge Magazine // Issue 81: Japan Blue

'Japan Blue' was coined by an English scholar who, visiting Japan in the 18th century, was struck by blue's overwhelming presence; from the archipelago to every doorway and everyone's clothes.

This issue journeys to the Okamoto Orifu Factory where master craftsmen work to satisfy the world's hunger for Japanese selvedge denim, and to the famous Water Masters who maintain the sukumo tradition that makes Japanese indigo darker and more intense than anywhere in the world.

Japan has a complex and obscure culture, and issue 81 of Selvedge Magazine begins to scratch the surface.
