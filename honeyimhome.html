Honey I'm Home // Writing on property, ownership and access

This book is a collection of idiosyncratic writings by fifteen authors, each responding to the meaning of property and the urban centre. Beginning at the home, each text explores what it means to own something through essays, poems, letters and an obituary, letting the book arrive at a collective understanding of access through class, race, money and more.

Editors:
