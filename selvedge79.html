Selvedge Magazine // Issue 79: Luna

From pocket springs in mattresses to the artwork of Astrid Krogh, this issue of Selvedge Magazine celebrates textiles that illuminate the shorter days and darker nights of winter.

Bohin pins can be found around the world; from the pinscreen animation to the intricate embroidery produced by Karen Nicol. This issue journeys to Nicol's London home as she prepares for a white Christmas, and travels to France to see the making of Bohin pins and see the glistening pinscreen films of Alexander Alexeieff and Claire Parker.

Issue 79 of Selvedge Magazine gets to the source of what brightens the lives of makers in the textile industry.
