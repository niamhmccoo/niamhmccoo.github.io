Selvedge Magazine // Issue 77: Chakra

2017 marked India's 70th anniversary of independence, and this issue of Selvedge Magazine celebrates the subcontinent's longstanding connection with textiles.

Issue 77 travels to the banks of the Sabarmati river where the air is perfect for spinning some of the world's finest thread. It explores the politics of the sari, and visits a family of carpet weavers followed by a village of traditional wood block carvers before delving into the turbulent history of Indian cotton.

From the architectural resilience of tarpaulin to appliqué and dyeing in Ahmedabad, Indian culture is embedded with textiles in every aspect of contemporary life.
